Claire Saffitz


About Claire

Claire Saffitz is an American food writer, chef, and YouTube personality who gained popularity through her appearances on the Bon Appétit YouTube channel. She starred in several series, including Gourmet Makes, where she created gourmet versions of popular snack foods. Saffitz was a contributing editor to Bon Appétit magazine until mid-2020. Since then, she has published two cookbooks, Dessert Person and What's for Dessert, both of which were New York Times Best Sellers. She continues to work as a video host on her own YouTube channel and as a freelance recipe developer for New York Times Cooking.
